Community Safety Unit

Welcome to the Community Safety Unit's Complaint Portal. The Community Safety Unit delivers a province-wide compliance and enforcement program to enhance public safety. The Community Safety Unit operates under the Cannabis Control and Licensing Act and the Community Safety Act. Its focus is on illegal unlicensed cannabis retailers and other related illegal operations, and properties regularly used for illegal activities.

The portal may be used to submit complaints to the Community Safety Unit under the Cannabis Control and Licensing Act and the Community Safety Act. The nature of your complaint will determine the complaint form that you will use.

Cannabis Control and Licensing Act

The Community Safety Unit is responsible for compliance and enforcement under the Cannabis Control and Licensing Act, with a focus on the illegal sale of cannabis. CSU investigators carry out compliance and enforcement activities against unlicensed cannabis retailers and other illegal sellers across the province.

If you would like to submit a complaint against an illegal unlicensed cannabis retailer or other seller, please select the option below.

Community Safety Act

The Community Safety Unit is also responsible for investigations under the Community Safety Act. The Community Safety Act targets properties in your community that are regularly used for specific criminal and nuisance activities, including drug production and trafficking, unlawful liquor sales, possession of unlawful weapons or explosives, and activities conducted by or on behalf of gangs and organized crime. CSU investigators carry out compliance and enforcement activities across the province against properties that receive complaints under the Community Safety Act.

If you would like to submit a complaint against a problem property that is regularly used for unlawful activity (possession/production/use/trafficking of illegal drugs/liquor, storage/possession of illegal firearms, gang or criminal organization activity, illegal gaming/gambling, storage/possession of stolen property) in a way that adversely affects your neighbourhood or community, please select the option below.
